Supplementary Data 3

Influence of different measures of size and GC content on chromosome and ER clustering

Supplementary Data 3 contains a collection of six-panel scatter plot figures for all bacterial phyla represented in the dataset with at least 100 extrachromosomal replicons (ERs). An additional figure includes replicons from all other phyla aggregated into a single panel for comparison.

Each figure displays all replicons from the given phylum, including both chromosomes and ERs. Replicons are plotted using different combinations of size and GC content measures to explore their distribution patterns in two-dimensional space.

Each figure is organized in a consistent 2 × 3 grid layout, where:

Columns represent three alternative measures of replicon size:
– absolute size (bp)
– % genome (%) (size relative to total genome size, %gen)
– % chromosome (%) (size relative to chromosome size, %chr)

Rows represent two measures of GC content:
– GC content (%)
– ΔGC (%) (difference between GC content of a replicon and GC content of the chromosome)
