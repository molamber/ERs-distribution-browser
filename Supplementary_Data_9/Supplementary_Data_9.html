Distribution of conjugation types among extrachromosomal replicons of Alphaproteobacteria, Betaproteobacteria and Gammaproteobacteria

This figure collection presents interactive plots showing extrachromosomal replicons (ERs) from three classes: Alphaproteobacteria, Betaproteobacteria, and Gammaproteobacteria.

Each ER is plotted by its size relative to the chromosome (%chr) and its GC content difference from the host chromosome (Î”GC, %). Points are colored by conjugation type, assigned using ConjScan (see Methods).

For each class, the total number of small and large ERs is indicated. The legend also shows the percentage of small and large ERs that belong to each conjugation category.
